New Patrolling system added!

Hello everyone. Today I'm going to share with you how we do the patrol system.

In The Awoken Hero, enemies will wander around and if you touch them, combat will be triggered.

Fayne fighting with a Skeleton.

In order to make the game more dynamic and interesting, now enemies can patrol.

Goblin Warrior patrolling.

Any enemy in the game what is called an awareness area.

Awareness area of a Goblin Thief.

If you enter this area, there is a good chance that the enemy will begin to think that there is something near him.

Then every enemy has a field of vision, if an enemy sees you they will start chasing you!

Field of view of a Cyclops.

Either way, you will start the battle if you get caught or you choose to be brave run into the enemies!

Combat with a Snail and a Goblin Warrior.

Some enemies run faster, some have a wider field of vision, some are smarter than others when it comes to knowing if someone is nearby.

That's all we have for the patrol at the moment. You can tell us what you think about the patrols, or combat in general, by sending a message to Twitter.

And you can also join our Discord to give feedback or talk with our lovely people.

That's it for now, see you around!

Magna.

Some of The Awoken Hero art!

Hello everyone! This time I will share some of the game art

I want to share with you something visual from The Awoken Hero. We've been busy for the last few months trying to find a tone for the game, and after that we started making enemies.

A tilemap for one of the first floors.

In The Awoken Hero, monster family becomes really important, you need to understand how families work and play around so you don't fall in battle.

The first family of the game is the Goblin family, they all have their own strengths and weaknesses, and they complement each other:

Some members of the Goblin's family.

The Shaman is usually in charge of healing and reinforcing his allies, while the Warrior, Mage, and Thief are more focused on inflicting damage on their prey.

In this world there are a lot of magical and dangerous creatures, one of which is the Cyclops, which can kill its enemies with a single blow.

If they are angry, you better run for your life:

Cyclops enraged.

That's all I wanted to say for today. In the next blog, we'll talk about the patrol system I'm implementing in the game, and a little bit about combat.

As always, you can follow me on Twitter. And you can also join our Discord to give feedback or talk with our lovely people.

That's it for now, see you around!
