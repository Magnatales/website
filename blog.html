Some of The Awoken Hero art!

Hello everyone! This time I will share some of the game art

This time I want to share with you something visual from The Awoken Hero. We've been busy for the last few months trying to find a tone for the game, and after that we started making enemies.

A tilemap for one of the first floors.

In The Awoken Hero, monster family becomes really important, you need to understand how families work and play around so you don't fall in battle.

The first family of the game is the Goblin family, they all have their own strengths and weaknesses, and they complement each other:

Some members of the Goblin's family.

The Shaman is usually in charge of healing and reinforcing his allies, while the Warrior, Mage, and Thief are more focused on inflicting damage on their prey.

In this world there are a lot of magical and dangerous creatures, one of which is the Cyclops, which can kill its enemies with a single blow.

If they are angry, you better run for your life:

Cyclops enraged.

That's all I wanted to say for today. In the next blog, we'll talk about the patrol system I'm implementing in the game, and a little bit about combat.

As always, you can follow me on Twitter. And you can also join our Discord to talk or give feedback to our lovely people.

That's it for now, see you around!

Welcome to the blog everyone!


Welcome everyone! And a little presentation about the project

My name is Luis "Magna" Jim√©nez. I'm a 29 year old Spanish developer who, at the time of writing this blog, has been learning Unity and C# for about 7 months.

Like everyone else, I started making little games and participating in every game jam I saw, and there was one particular jam where I made a little prototype with a story I had in mind. But that's not where the story started.

Fayne in the combat pose.

I always liked video games, especially RPGs, and from time to time I liked to think about a world, a world where you control a girl, this girl has white hair and she's going through an ordeal, her life wasn't easy at all.

Now this girl is called Fayne, she is a strong warrior from a tribe, a tribe of fighters.

I have the opportunity and the tools to create this world, this story, these characters, and I will not waste the time. If you're interested in the project, you can join this journey with me via this blog or on my Twitter.

We've also recently set up a Discord channel and would love to have you there. I'm very open about feedback and love to hear your opinions.

That's all for now, see you around!
